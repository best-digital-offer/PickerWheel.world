Technical Issues

If you're experiencing technical problems, try refreshing your browser, clearing your cache, or using a different browser. Our tool works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Feature Requests

We love hearing ideas for new features! Send us your suggestions and we'll consider them for future updates.

Educational Use

Teachers and educators are welcome to use Random Picker Wheel in their classrooms. The tool is completely free and requires no registration.

Commercial Use

Businesses and organizations can use our tool for events, team building, and promotional activities at no cost.
