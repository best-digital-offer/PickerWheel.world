Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2024

Information We Collect

Random Picker Wheel operates as a client-side application. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. All wheel configurations, coin flip results, random number generations, and data remain in your browser and are not transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we don't use, share, or sell any personal data. The wheel spinner, coin flip tool, random number generator, and all selection tools operate entirely in your browser using local processing.

Cookies and Local Storage

Our website may use browser local storage to temporarily save your wheel configurations for your convenience. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or third parties.

Third-Party Services

We may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us improve our service. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data and does not identify individual users.

Data Security

Since all processing happens in your browser, your data remains secure on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect our website infrastructure.

Children's Privacy

Our service is safe for children as we don't collect any personal information. Teachers and parents can use our wheel spinner, coin flip simulator, random number generator, and name picker tools with confidence knowing no student data is collected or stored.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our website.
